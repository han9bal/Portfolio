Courses

Layla offers certain types of voice exercises, masterclasses and Academy preperation

Opera Solo Singer Master Class for Students at Opera and Music Academies

In this Opera Solo Singing Master Class, the student will enter a unique program, which is designed to help them develop their own unique and authentic voice, to boost their confidence and support their growth of self in their performance. The fundament of the class is Italian style Bel Canto and classical singing technique including breathing, musical terminology, diction and phrasing. The students are provided with clear stepwise learning objectives. The passion for music is brought to live using personal feedback. Finally in the class, we will engage with community though visit at cultural institutions and prepare for the students’ own concerts.
Duration: 3-7 days.

Voice and song coaching

Training your voice can be a wonderful experience. Follow a few private lessons in voice coaching with full attention on how you use your voice and you will discover how easily your voice can be significantly improved. In the lessons you will do specially designed exercises, which will expand the control over your voice. Improving the way you use your voice will pay off every time you stand in front of an audience as a singer, an actor or you just would like your message to come through.

Preparation for Music Academy Entrance

Join my preparation program for the entrance exam to Music College or Music Conservatorium. I have a 100% pass rate for my students. I developed the program in 2017 at Utventa College in Ukraine. In the program I:
- uncover the students unique and authentic voice, build confidence and working diligently to bring passion for music to live,
- provide the students with clear learning objectives and make use of detailed assessment plans, and
- organise performances and concerts where the students get the possibility to stand in front of an audience, as well as trips to cultural institutions, including theatres and concert halls.

Rudersdal Summer Concerts

In august 2022, Leila Sherzai gave a concert at Søllerød Sognegård promoted by Rudersdal Summer Concerts. The concert was sold out long in advance, and she received standing ovations from an enthusiastic audience that included children and adults of all ages. The Ukrainian ambassador also attended the concert. It has been a huge enrichment to have Leila Shezai in the program for Rudersdal Summer Concerts and it will support our ambitions to make Rudersdal Summer Concerts internationally recognised.

Christine Pryn, Director Rudersdal Summer Concerts
